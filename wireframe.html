FAQ

Q: Is this a technical guide about deploying a token contract?
A: No. This is a cultural and community guide. It assumes you've solved the technical side (or have someone who has). VESSEL is about the other 80% of the launch that most founders ignore until it's too late: narrative, community building, content, and the first 30 days of showing up.
Q: Does this work if I'm a solo founder without a team?
A: Yes, and in some ways it's more useful solo. VESSEL is built around what one focused person can actually execute. The community phases scale from small (5 founding members) to large — start where you are.
Q: How do I use this? Is it a PDF or a Notion template?
A: It's a Notion template. You'll receive a link to duplicate the page directly into your own Notion workspace. From there it's yours — edit, fill in, delete what doesn't apply. No login required beyond a standard free Notion account.
